# TimeTravel
TimeTraval – AI-powered expense predictor and financial goal tracker. SpendSense helps users take control of their money by predicting future expenses, detecting overspending habits, and helping achieve long-term financial goals like car down payments or travel savings.
## 🚀 Overview
Instead of tracking spending after it happens, TimeTravel uses AI to predict upcoming expenses based on your habits, timing, and goals.

## 🎯 Features
- Add daily expenses manually (no CSVs)
- Predict future spending trends
- Daily/weekly/monthly analysis
- Goal tracker (“You’re 80% to your dream car!”)
- Smart alerts and saving tips
